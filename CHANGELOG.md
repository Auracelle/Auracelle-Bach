## Release Notes

### Version 1.0.0 - "Foundation Release"

This inaugural release establishes Auracelle Bach as a comprehensive AI governance intelligence suite. The system integrates advanced mathematical methods with behavioral science frameworks to support evidence-based policy analysis and institutional decision-making.

**Key Highlights**:
- Complete mathematical intelligence suite with 9 enhancement categories
- Behavioral science integration across 9 dimensions
- Live API connectivity to major governance data sources
- Production-ready deployment options (Colab, local, cloud)
- Comprehensive documentation for researchers and practitioners
- Open-source release under MIT License

**Target Users**:
- Academic researchers in AI governance
- Policy analysts in government and international organizations
- Think tank researchers and strategic intelligence professionals
- Wargaming practitioners and scenario planners
- Graduate students in governance and policy studies

**Research Applications**:
- Multi-stakeholder governance analysis
- Policy stress testing and robustness evaluation
- Evidence synthesis from multiple data sources
- Institutional theory computational modeling
- Wargaming scenario development and analysis

**Next Steps**:
- Community feedback and feature prioritization
- Performance optimization for large-scale datasets
- Additional API integrations based on user needs
- Enhanced documentation and tutorials
- Academic publication of methodology
